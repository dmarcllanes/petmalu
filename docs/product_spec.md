# Product Specification

## Product Name
AI Pet Nutrition & Wellness SaaS

## Positioning

A web-based feeding optimization and wellness tracking platform for healthy pets.

This product:
- Calculates daily calorie needs
- Optimizes food portions
- Tracks weight trends
- Monitors ingredient allergies
- Manages supplement reminders

This product does NOT:
- Diagnose medical conditions
- Provide veterinary advice
- Generate therapeutic diets

## Target Users

- Urban pet owners
- Multi-pet households
- Data-conscious pet parents

## Core Features

1. Calorie Calculator
2. Portion Optimizer
3. Weight Tracking Dashboard
4. Allergy Ingredient Scanner
5. Supplement Reminder System

## Tier Structure

### Free
- 1 pet
- Basic calorie calculation
- Manual weight logging

### Pro
- Up to 5 pets
- Auto calorie adjustment
- Weight analytics
- Allergy scanner
- Supplement reminders
- Treat tracking

### Premium
- Unlimited pets
- Growth projections
- Calorie simulation
- PDF reports
- Advanced analytics

## Platform

Mobile-first web-based SaaS.
