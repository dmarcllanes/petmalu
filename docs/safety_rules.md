# Safety Rules

This product is health-adjacent but not medical.

## Calorie Engine

- Must use rule-based formula:
  RER = 70 × (body weight kg ^ 0.75)
- Apply safe multipliers.
- Must enforce minimum and maximum safe calorie thresholds.
- Puppy growth floor must be enforced.

## Restrictions

- No therapeutic diet generation.
- No disease-based diet recommendations.
- No diagnosis.
- No supplement dosing recommendations.

## Adjustment Limits

- Calorie adjustments limited to ±10%.
- Auto adjustments must require user confirmation.

## Medical Conditions

If pet has:
- Kidney disease
- Heart disease
- Liver disease
- Diabetes

Advanced features must be blocked.

Display:
"Please consult a licensed veterinarian."
